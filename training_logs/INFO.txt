NeTab Training Logs Directory
=============================

This directory contains training monitoring and log files.

What Gets Saved Here:
--------------------
After running training_stage.py, you'll find:

- Training log files (training_YYYYMMDD_HHMMSS.log)
- Training history JSON files
- Loss tracking and progress information

Files Created:
--------------
- *.log files: Detailed training progress and metrics
- training_history.json: Loss curves and learning rates
- Timestamp-based naming to track multiple training runs

Use these files to monitor training progress and debug issues.
